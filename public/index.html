About Me

Hi! I’m Dr. Sue Tarazi and I am a full stack C# .NET and JavaScript Software Engineer with a passion for making life better. After earning my undergraduate degree in Biomedical Engineering, I worked as a dot.commer for two years until the bubble burst and there were no tech jobs to be had.

I then returned to graduate school and got my doctorate degree in Physical Therapy. Since then, I have owned and operated my own Physical Therapy practice for over 9 years that operated in the black every year. I really enjoy problem solving, how things fit together and connecting with others – all skill transferable to the tech world. I also bring with me the skills of organization and running a business.

Tech allows me to reach a broader audience. Now, instead of just improving the life of one patient at a time, using tech and working with a team allows me to develop a product or service that would benefit many people at once and be more impactful.
